Info





The UAAG Alliance (United Airlines Association Global) is an alliance originating from AM4 (airline4.net). The alliance is a few months old. There are three admins:
IV International Airways (The founder and CEO)
Orbit vAirlines (The COO)
Shorts Stream (The CPAO)

The alliance has many members and is quickly growing in size and value.





Weekly Newsletter




May 25, 2020 newsletter part 1>

Attention members of the UAAG,

We have some good and bad news coming in this newsletter so please read it.

Good news is we have some new positions for admin positions with in the UAAG. PRO, public relations officer, is in charge of sending out newsletters, like this one your reading, to airlines of the UAAG alliance.
This position has paused and is on hold. Admin will open applications on May 30, 2020. The first person to fill out the application will be selected. We have had no one try to apply. If no one applies admin may just choose an alliance member. More positions coming.


Please stay active. Try to depart routes regularly. Its okay if you miss a day or two. If you have problems with departing routes please let an admin member know.

I would also like someone to create a logo for the UAAG. I have been thinking of making a simple website so everyone has access to the constitution, rules, discord link, etc. If you are wanting to make a logo for the UAAG you can send a picture of the finished product on discord, or on Instagram (DM me @iv.aviation.14).
Requirements for logo:
-must have the letters UAAG
-must be aviation related
-the rest is up to you
-No deadline
If you have questions about the logo send me a message.

Please follow me on Instagram. My old account got deleted, so if you were following iv_aviation_14 make sure to follow iv.aviation.14 because this is my new account. You can also follow me on YouTube. IV AVIATION. more videos are coming.

Part 2:

Finally it’s time for the bad news. The UAAG has been struggling to keep airlines to stay with the UAAG. I’m not mentioning names but we lost airlines yesterday. This resulted in the value and our rank going down (dramatically).
As a result admin lowered the minimum IPO from $10.00 to $5.00 to help attract airlines. If you are going to leave please let and admin member know.

If you would like to help the UAAG please contact your investors. This is so we can resume our normal rank and value. You can contact investors by clicking the envelope icon in the top
right hand corner of your screen. Next click compose or write a message. Next you have the options to click airlines of all investors. The number of your investors may vary. Click investors and ask them nicely to join the UAAG.

Thanks for reading and if you have questions regarding this newsletter contact me directly in the AM4 app, Instagram, or discord.
